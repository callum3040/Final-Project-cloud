Characters

Daryl Lorenz

A Zeon Chief Petty Officer and ace sniper. He carries with him a retro-style radio he has owned since childhood that plays pop oldies. As an infantryman, Daryl lost his legs from mortar fire during a landing raid on an enemy colony in the first half of the One Year War.

Io Fleming

A Federation  Ensign who enjoys pounding his drum sticks on his mobile suit cockpit console to  free jazz music on his pirate radio receiver. Prior to the One Year War, Io, along with his friends Claudia Peer and Cornelius Qaqa, participated in  air racing events in Side 4. His father, who was Mayor of Side 4, had committed suicide during the outbreak of the war.
